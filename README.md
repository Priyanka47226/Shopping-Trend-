
## SHOPPING TREND ANALYSIS

The goal of the project is to extract actionable insights that can help businesses improve sales strategies, customer engagement, and personalization.

This dataset contains customer-level shopping behavior data, including demographic details, purchase information, payment preferences, and shopping frequency.
The dataset is useful for data analysis, data science projects, machine learning models, and business insights such as customer segmentation, purchase pattern analysis, and recommendation systems.
## Dataset Description


Dataset Name: Shopping Trend

File Format: CSV

Total Records: 3900

Total Features: 19

Data Type: Structured tabular data

The dataset includes customer information such as age, gender, purchased items, category, purchase amount, payment method, shipping type, discounts, and purchase frequency.
## Key Features


- Customer demographics: Age, Gender, Location

- Purchase details: Item Purchased, Category, Size, Color, Season

- Financial data: Purchase Amount (USD), Discount Applied, Promo Code Used

- Customer behavior: Previous Purchases, Frequency of Purchases

- Preferences: Payment Method, Preferred Payment Method, Shipping Type

- Feedback: Review Rating, Subscription Status
## Key Insights

* Clothing is the most frequently purchased category

* Customers with subscriptions tend to purchase more frequently

* Credit Card and PayPal are the most commonly used payment methods

* Seasonal variations significantly impact purchase behavior

* Higher previous purchases often correlate with higher purchase frequency
